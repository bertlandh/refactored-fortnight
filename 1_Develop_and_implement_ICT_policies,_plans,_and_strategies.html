    Develop and implement ICT policies, plans, and strategies:

    • Steps:
      1. Conduct a thorough review of existing ICT policies and procedures.
      2. Identify gaps and areas for improvement based on industry standards and organizational goals.
      3. Formulate new policies and strategies to address identified needs.
      4. Document policies, plans, and strategies in consultation with relevant stakeholders.
      5. Obtain approval from management and disseminate policies to the team.
      6. Ensure understanding and compliance through training and communication.

     

    • Team Utilization:
      • Involve the entire ICT team in policy review and formulation to gather insights and ensure buy-in.
      • Assign specific team members to research and draft policy documents.
      • Conduct training sessions led by team members to ensure understanding and compliance.

     

    • KPIs and Project Completion:
      • Track completion of policy documents against established timelines.
      • Monitor adherence to policies through periodic audits and feedback mechanisms.
      • Measure staff compliance and understanding through assessments and feedback surveys.

    • Start Date: April 1st
    • Duration: Continuous process throughout the year with milestones for policy review, development, and implementation.

     

    This cycle of review, development, and implementation will continue throughout the year with milestones for each phase. The process is continuous to ensure that policies are regularly reviewed and updated to align with changing organizational needs and industry standards.

     

    KPIs and Project Completion:

    • Track completion of policy documents against established timelines.
    • Monitor adherence to policies through periodic audits and feedback mechanisms.
    • Measure staff compliance and understanding through assessments and feedback surveys.

     

    Throughout the year, regular assessments and audits will be conducted to ensure that policies are effectively implemented and adhered to by the team.
